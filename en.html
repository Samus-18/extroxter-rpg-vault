How to install

Every card lists compatibility separately. Do not use a JAR made for another Minecraft version or loader.

1

Choose a project

Find a mod or shader and click the green Choose version button.

2

Select a build

Choose the exact Minecraft version and loader. Planned ports are visible but cannot be downloaded.

3

Download

Click Download. Mods belong in mods; shader packs belong in shaderpacks.
